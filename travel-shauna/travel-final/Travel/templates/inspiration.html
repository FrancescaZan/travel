Feeling inspired? Find your result on our list below...

Affordability – Beach
Albufeira, Portugal

The largest, liveliest and most energetic of all of the resort towns that line southern Portugal's beautiful Algarve coastline. Stunning beaches, a glorious climate, varied eating and a buzzing nightlife, all at an affordable price. A large variety of reasonable hotels reside just a short walk from the coast and offer a range of cheap activities and attractions.

Luxury – Beach
Indian Shores, Florida

Located along the Gulf of Mexico, Indian Shores is a beautiful stretch of beach with white sparkling sand in the Florida sun. The fewer crowd makes it an ideal destination to watch the Florida sunset in peace. With plenty of options for fine dining, boat rentals, designer fashion and water sports, you can plan your holiday according to your own personal preference.

Affordability – Nature
Teide National Park, Spain

Visit the biggest park in the Canary Islands, centred on Mount Teide, the highest mountain of Spain. This Mars-like landscape has a range of spectacular views and activities such as quad biking, jeep safaris and paragliding. If you want to appreciate the beautiful outdoors on a budget, then Teide National Park should be one on your list.

Luxury – Nature
Vatnajokull National Park, Iceland

In Southeast Iceland, Vatnajokull is the largest National Park in Europe. It features some of the most extreme and beautiful nature highlights in the world, including the largest glacier in Europe, stunning peaks, cascading waterfalls, gorgeous hiking trails and an awe inspiring glacial river canyon. And after roughing it in the wild outdoors, come back to one of the many elegant and indulgent hotels located in the surrounding area.

Affordability – Ski
La Plague, France

Like skiing? Then visit the French Alps, the most stunning mountain range in Europe, with a good selection of beginner pistes and great choices for the more advanced skier and snowboarder. This huge area of resorts and villages make La Plague a true playground for lovers of the snowy outdoors, both in summer and winter. The good news is that you don’t have to mortgage your house in order to enjoy these slopes, there are a range of affordable options that will take you by surprise.

Luxury – Ski
Geneva, Switzerland

Geneva is an ideal base from which to explore the charming lakeside towns of Lausanne and Montreux, the beauty that is the Swiss Alps and the glitz and glamour of St Moritz. With boutique wellness and spa hotels, private luxury cottages and alpine chalet lodgings, you have a full range of lavish options to consider.

Affordability – Nightlife
Malia, Greece

Move over Ibiza, Malia is the Mediterranean’s new party capital. If you’re ready to party from sundown to sunrise, the Malia Strip is the place for you. Found behind the town’s main beach, it’s stuffed full of bars, pubs and clubs, offering partygoers a fantastic night out. Drinks, accommodation and entry all at unbeatable prices that won’t break the bank.

Luxury – Nightlife
Las Vegas, Nevada

Known as the ultimate playground, Las Vegas is both colourful and vibrant, making it an ideal destination for travellers looking for late night entertainment and a buzzing nightlife scene. From the famous Vegas strip to wild pool parties, uncover the excitement of Sin City in style. The city also takes very good care of its visitors, offering countless grand five star hotels, glamourous restaurants, casinos and spa destinations so you can party in luxury.

Affordability – Culture
Rome, Italy

visit Italy’s capital, a sprawling, cosmopolitan city with nearly 3,000 years of globally influential art, architecture and culture on display. See ancient ruins such as the Forum and the Colosseum, while staying in one of Rome’s many affordable hotels. A heady mix of haunting ruins, awe-inspiring art and vibrant street life, Italy’s hot-blooded capital is one of the world’s most romantic and charismatic cities. So not to fear, you can definitely journey into the past on a budget.

Luxury – Culture
Cairo, Egypt

This sprawling capital is set on the Nile River. At its heart is Tahrir Square and the vast Egyptian Museum, a trove of antiquities including royal mummies and gilded King Tutankhamun artefacts. Nearby, Giza is the site of the iconic pyramids and Great Sphinx, dating to the 26th century BC. Soak up the past while staying in fancy modern lodgings complete with river views, high quality dining and ready advice on the best historical sites and monuments in the area.
